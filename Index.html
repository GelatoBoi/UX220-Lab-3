The Best Thing I've Read: by Areeb Mohsin

Personally, the best book that I've read recently in a long time was Advanced Functions 12: Study Guide and University Handbook. Written by Laurissa Werhun and Paula Thiessen.

This book has a special place in my heart, as it was the one of the few books in my life that brought out the most emotion in me.

Most high schools students can also agree with my sentiment for this piece of literature. I hope that many other future generations of students may also experience the wonderful journey this book brings readers on.
